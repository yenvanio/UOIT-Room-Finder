F.A.Q

What does Room Finder do?

Room Finder uses the schedules created at the beginning of the semester to determine when a room is not being used by any classes.

What doesn't Room Finder do?

Room Finder does not provide live updates and sometimes a room may be booked by clubs, societies or other organizations which Room Finder doesn't take into account.

How do I use Room Finder?

The ( Home home ) page has a list of current open rooms and you can choose to search by ( Roommeeting_room ) or ( Timeaccess_time ) from the navigation menu. By clicking on an open room you will be able to see when the next class is and where the building is located on the map.
